About Rigour

What this
studio is for

Rigour builds AI tools for people doing serious professional work. Not demos, not experiments — tools that earn their place in a real workflow.

The standard for every product is the same: does it make the work better, faster, or more defensible? If it does not, it does not ship.

Every tool in the Rigour suite is built by someone who has spent years inside large organisations and knows exactly what bad AI output costs when the stakes are real.

Principle 01
Evidence before rhetoric

Every claim in every output must be traceable to a source, a decision, or an observed fact. No management-journal register.

Principle 02
Tools that accumulate

The best tools get more valuable the more you use them. Memory, context, and history are features, not footnotes.

Principle 03
No vague outputs

If the AI cannot name the decision-maker, the constraint, and the consequence of inaction — it has not finished the job.

Principle 04
Small studio, long game

One tool built well beats five tools built fast. Rigour does not ship until it is ready for someone who cannot afford for it not to work.
